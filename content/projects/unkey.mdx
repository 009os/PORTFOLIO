**_Funding Fee Arbitrage_** is a production-grade trading system designed to automate delta-neutral strategies and consistently capture positive funding rates across major crypto exchanges like **_Binance_**, **_Bybit_**, and **_OKX_**. The system actively monitors funding rates, places offsetting positions, and dynamically manages trades based on user-defined configurations.

## Performance Metrics

- **Monthly Trading Volume**: 500+ million USDT
- **System Uptime**: 99.9% availability
- **Execution Latency**: Sub-100ms average response time
- **Daily Trades**: 15,000+ arbitrage transactions
- **Funding Rate Capture**: 85%+ of available opportunities
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: 12-18% annualized yield
- **Cross-Exchange Coverage**: 3 major exchanges (Binance, Bybit, OKX)
- **Supported Assets**: 25+ cryptocurrency pairs including BTC, ETH, SOL, ADA

## Technical Architecture

Built entirely with **_NestJS_**, the system features a modular microservices architecture designed for high-frequency trading:

- **Real-time Data Processing**: WebSocket feeds from all 3 exchanges with under 50ms latency
- **Intelligent Order Routing**: Dynamic order placement based on liquidity and funding rate differentials
- **Risk Management**: Automated position sizing and stop-loss mechanisms
- **Database Layer**: **_Prisma ORM_** for reliable data persistence with PostgreSQL
- **Caching Layer**: **_Redis_** for ultra-fast in-memory operations and queue management
- **API Integration**: Both **_REST_** and **_WebSocket APIs_** for live order execution

## Advanced Features

- **Delta-Neutral Positioning**: Maintains market-neutral exposure across all positions
- **Dynamic Rebalancing**: Automatic position adjustment based on funding rate changes
- **Multi-Exchange Arbitrage**: Simultaneous execution across Binance, Bybit, and OKX
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Comprehensive dashboards for P&L tracking and risk metrics
- **Automated Compliance**: Built-in regulatory reporting and audit trails

## Trading Strategy

The system employs sophisticated arbitrage strategies:

1. **Funding Rate Arbitrage**: Captures funding rate differentials between exchanges
2. **Cross-Exchange Spread Trading**: Exploits price inefficiencies across platforms
3. **Delta-Neutral Hedging**: Maintains balanced exposure regardless of market direction
4. **Dynamic Position Sizing**: Adjusts position sizes based on volatility and liquidity
