**Multi Leg Options** is a production-grade options trading system engineered to execute volatility-based, multi-leg strategies across global derivatives markets. 

## Performance Metrics

- **Monthly Trading Volume**: 1 billion USDT
- **Market Share**: 7% of Binance Global Options volume
- **Daily Contracts**: 50,000+ options contracts processed
- **Execution Latency**: Sub-millisecond order placement
- **System Uptime**: 99.95% availability
- **Supported Assets**: BTC, ETH, SOL, ADA options
- **Expiry Coverage**: 7-day to 3-month expiries
- **Strike Coverage**: 20+ strike prices per expiry

## Technical Architecture

Built entirely in **NestJS**, the system features a high-frequency trading architecture:

- **Real-time Order Book Processing**: Live feeds from Binance with under 100ms latency
- **Multi-Leg Order Execution**: Simultaneous placement across multiple expiries and strikes
- **Intelligent Order Routing**: Dynamic order placement based on liquidity and volatility
- **Advanced Risk Management**: Real-time position monitoring and automated hedging
- **Database Layer**: **Prisma ORM** with PostgreSQL for reliable data persistence
- **Caching Layer**: **Redis** for ultra-fast in-memory operations and order queuing
- **Analytics Engine**: **InfluxDB** for time-series performance analysis

## Advanced Trading Strategies

The system employs sophisticated options strategies:

### **2-Way Delta-Neutral Hedging**
- **Primary Strategy**: Options-based delta hedging for optimal exposure
- **Backup Strategy**: Automatic futures hedging when options orders are delayed
- **Continuous Exposure**: Never leaves positions unhedged
- **Dynamic Rebalancing**: Real-time position adjustment based on market movements

### **Delta Scalping**
- **Dynamic Hedging**: Adjusts delta exposure based on underlying price movements


### **IV-Driven Directional Bias**
- **Implied Volatility Analysis**: Real-time IV surface modeling
- **Volatility Arbitrage**: Captures IV differentials across strikes and expiries
- **Directional Positioning**: Adjusts strategy based on IV skew and term structure

## Risk Management Features

- **Real-time P&L Tracking**: Live profit/loss monitoring across all positions
- **Position Limits**: Automated position sizing based on volatility and liquidity
